Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

At A.R.N Growth Charitable Trust, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or make a donation.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Fill out a contact form
  • Make a donation
  • Sign up for our newsletter
  • Volunteer with our organization

This information may include:

  • Name and contact information
  • Payment details (processed securely via Razorpay)
  • Communication preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process your donations and provide receipts
  • Send you updates about our programs and impact
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except as described below:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our operations.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect your ability to use some features of our website.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
